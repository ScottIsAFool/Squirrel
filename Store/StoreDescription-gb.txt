Squirrel is a fully featured Pocket (formerly Read It Later) client for Windows Phone 8.

Trial version and paid version are *identical*, paid just shows support for the app.

Features:
- Offline reading
- Manage articles (delete/archive/favourite)
- Manage article tags
- Live tile showing article counts
- Different layouts for showing your articles (list/box)
- Customisable article viewing: change font size, theme, justified text
- Have Squirrel read out the article to you.
